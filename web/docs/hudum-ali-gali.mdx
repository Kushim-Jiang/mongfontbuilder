## Phonetic letters

<LetterTable locale={locale} />

**Final <Mong locale="MNGx" links="A G" /> in letter <Mong locale="MNGx" links="ng" />**. Both final <Mong locale="MNGx" links="N G4" /> and final <Mong locale="MNGx" links="A G4" /> are used to record Tibetan /ŋ/ in _Buddhist Mantras_. The final <Mong locale="MNGx" links="A G4" /> is considered as a variant of final <Mong locale="MNGx" links="A G" />, and the two shapes correspond to the same written form.
