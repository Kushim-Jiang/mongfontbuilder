## Phonetic letters

<LetterTable locale={locale} />

**Final <Mong written="AG" /> in letter _ŋ_**. Both final <Mong written="NG4" /> and final <Mong written="AG4" /> are used to record Tibetan /ŋ/ in _Buddhist Mantras_. The final <Mong written="AG4" /> is considered as a variant of final <Mong written="AG" />, and the two shapes correspond to the same written form.
