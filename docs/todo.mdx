**Letter <Mong locale="TOD" links="ny" />**. The letter <Mong locale="TOD" links="ny" /> is introduced in [蒙汉词典 (增订本)](https://search.worldcat.org/zh-cn/title/867334994), but is not found in Todo literature. **Function of the sign <Mong locale="TOD" links="ƚ" />**. The sign <Mong locale="TOD" links="ƚ" /> behaves as a base character in some cases, but its logic in the shaping process behaves as a combining mark. For this reason, the necessary preprocessing and postprocessing should be introduced: before the Mongolian-specific steps begin, <Mong locale="TOD" links="ƚ" /> is shaped in mark class, modifying the joining type of the characters before and after <Mong locale="TOD" links="ƚ" />; after the Mongolian-specific steps finish, those signs <Mong locale="TOD" links="ƚ" /> that need to be represented as marks are shaped to ligature with the neighboring letters. These extra steps are not included in the condition table shown above, but are completed in the specific implementation.
